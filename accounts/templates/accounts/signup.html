About Webapp

Hello there, welcome to the money tracker web home page. This is a web app that helps you track your daily expenses. The motive for developing this website was due to the fact that a lot or let's say enough people hardly save money, thereby overspending more than they should be spending out of their earnings or daily income. In order to reduce amount of money spent daily on expenses, you should consider using this app. All you have to do is create a budget for the most important expenses. There are default categories of expense to select from and your expenses would be tracked.
